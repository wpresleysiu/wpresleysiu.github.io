Brisket

    Ingredients

  • Brisket

    USDA Choice or better

  • Kosher Salt

  • Course Black Pepper

  • Other Seasonings

    Get creative.

Start with a cold brisket. Trim off the large chunk of fat on the side, and trim down the fat cap to about 1/8 inch. Then trim the edges of the brisket and try to get as close to an even rectangular shape as you can. Cover the entire brisket in yellow mustard, and add your seasonings.

If you're using an offset smoker, start a bed of charcoal on one side of your firebox and get your smoker up to 225Â° F. Place a few chunks of unlit wood on the other side of the firebox. This will help dry out the wood so it is ready to burn. Get a shallow water pan and place it in the cook chamber close to the heat source.

Place the brisket on the smoker with the point closer to the heat source.
